Voice Care Workshop

A special program to protect teachers' voices, improve their health, and train them with new skills for better teaching.

Teacher Health & Training

First-of-its-Kind
Voice Care for Teachers

Teachers speak for hours every single day. This program is designed to protect and care for their most important tool — their voice.

Protecting the Voice

Teachers use their voice for hours every day. This program treats them as "Professional Voice Users" and helps them keep their voice healthy and strong for a long time.

Finding Voice Problems Early

Teachers are trained to notice small changes in their voice. This helps them catch any voice problem early — before it becomes serious or causes lasting damage.

Overall Health & Good Workplace

Along with voice care, this program also looks at making the classroom and workplace more comfortable and healthy for teachers across Maharashtra.

Career Advancement Scheme (C.A.S.)

Modern Skills Training
for Teachers

Working with MSFDA and Madan Mohan Malaviya Faculty Development Academy to give teachers useful, practical skills.

Emotional Intelligence

Helping teachers understand and manage feelings — their own and their students'. This makes teaching easier and the classroom a better place for everyone.

Design Thinking

Teaching teachers to think in new and creative ways to solve problems in class and make their lessons more interesting for students.

AI Tools Training

Teaching teachers how to use AI tools to save time, reduce extra work, and bring modern technology into the classroom.

Key Results

What This Program
Will Achieve

Real, visible changes that happen when teachers are trained well and their health is properly taken care of.

Better Connection in Class

When teachers communicate better, students feel more comfortable asking questions and taking part in class. This builds their confidence.

Better Teaching Quality

With proper training and state support, teaching quality improves — even in colleges in small towns and rural areas of Maharashtra.

Teamwork and Responsibility

Principals and teachers work together with full dedication. This teamwork helps the program reach its goals and makes a real difference.
